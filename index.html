Website Plan: Discover Palawan


Site Name

Name:Discover Palawan:The Best Naturally Island

This name reflects the Palawan's legendary status as the Philippines's cleanest, greenest ecological frontier.

Optional Domain discoverpalawan.ph

Site Purpose

The Website will serve as the comprehensive digitall guide for domestic or international tourist looking to explore Palawan.

Scenarios

  • What is the top tourist attraction in Palawan?
  • How can I learn more about Palawan's Festival and traditional food?

Color scheme

This colors reflect the breathtaking aquatic beauty,pristine nature, and warm tropical sunshine of the province.

  • Ocean Teal: Used for headings and navigation(represent the crystal-clear waters.)

  • Sand Beige: Used for Buttons,accents, and highlight(to capture the essence of pristine shores, bright sunshine, and welcoming locals).

Typography

  • Roboto:Used for body and content(eco-friendly).

  • Monserrat:Used for headings and titles(looks clean and easy to read).
